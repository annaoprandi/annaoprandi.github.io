  • Galaxy quenching from cosmic web detachment (Aragon-Calvo et al., 2019)
  • Rapid environmental quenching of satellite dwarf galaxies in the Local Group (Wetzel et al., 2018)

Extras

If you would like to read more about the subject here are some great resources:

Advanced:

  • The Connection between Galaxies and their Dark Matter Halos (Wechsler et al., 2018): very comprehensive review of the connection between CDM haloes and galaxies, including techniques and applications.
  • ELVIS: Exploring the Local Volume in Simulations (Kimmel et al., 2013): cosmological simulation that reproduces the conditions of the Local Group.
  • MultiDark simulations: the story of dark matter halos and density profiles (Klypin et al., 2016) : cosmological simulations that reproduce the universe on a large scale
  • Quenching timescales of galaxies in the EAGLE simulations (Wright et al, 2018): a different approach to quenching timescales.



Basic:

  • Simulated evolution of the MW dark matter halo: movie of the formation of the Milky Way CDM halo (simulated).
  • Dark Matter and Galaxy rotation : why do we think dark matter exists?
